Pillar 1

Tech & Digital Innovation

Students receive structured training in programming, web development, and digital creation. The programme is hands-on from day one — no passive learning, only building.

Each participant works toward a capstone project: a real digital product they design, build, and present. Facilitators guide but the creativity belongs entirely to the student.

Pillar 2

Football League

A fully structured competitive football programme running parallel boys and girls leagues. Not just a kickabout — a proper league with fixtures, standings, and a finals week.

Football teaches what sport uniquely can: how to handle defeat, lead from the back, and win with grace.
