Privacy

Privacy Policy

How THRIVE protects enquiries, donor details, participant data, media, and admin-managed content.

Last updated: June 2026. This policy should be reviewed by counsel before production launch.

What we collect

We only request information needed to respond to enquiries, coordinate volunteers or sponsors, acknowledge donations, run programmes, and protect participants. This may include name, email, interest type, message content, donation reference details a donor chooses to share, and quiz/leaderboard display names.

Donor privacy

Donation records are never published with full bank references, phone numbers, email addresses, or private notes. Public acknowledgements use only an opt-in display name such as "Anonymous", first name only, or an organisation name approved by the donor.

  • Donor confirmations are collected through a private email flow or secure backend form — never stored in browser public storage.
  • We use a donation reference ID for internal reconciliation, not your personal details.
  • Explicit consent is required before publishing any donor name.
  • Donor records are deleted or anonymised when no longer needed for accounting or legal retention.

Local storage and admin tools

The current static website may store admin edits, quiz results, and league standings in browser storage. That storage is not a secure database and does not hold private donor identities or participant personal data. Admin authentication uses a session-only PIN that clears when the browser tab is closed.

Analytics and cookies

The site does not use third-party advertising trackers or social media pixels by default. If analytics are added in future, THRIVE will publish the provider names, purpose, data retention period, and any consent controls required by applicable law. Any cookies used are functional only (session management, user preferences).

Media and photographs

Photos or videos of programme participants are published only with appropriate consent. Images may be removed promptly on receipt of a valid takedown request sent to our email address below. Stock images from Unsplash are used under their licence where real programme photos are unavailable.

Data security

We take reasonable technical steps to protect information in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. No system is 100% secure; we will notify affected parties promptly if a data breach occurs that affects their personal information.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you. Contact us at the address below and we will respond within 30 days.

Contact

For privacy questions, donor anonymity requests, or media takedown requests, contact hello@thrivefoundation.org.
