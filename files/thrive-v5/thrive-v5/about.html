About THRIVE

An independent youth development initiative equipping young Nigerians with the skills, confidence, and mindset to grow.

Our Story

Born from a belief that every young person deserves to thrive

Our initiative started with a simple but powerful conviction: that young people are capable of extraordinary things when given the right environment, challenge, and support. Our maiden edition took place at FGC NISE, Anambra State in November 2025 — combining technology training, competitive football, and inspirational sessions into one coherent programme.

The response was immediate and overwhelming. Students stepped into challenges they had never faced, discovered abilities they did not know they had, and left with a confidence that extended far beyond the competition arena.

We are an independent organisation. 2026 is A Time To Build — expanding from one venue to multiple schools across Nigeria.

"Growth arrives in stages — small inches that stack up into miles. Invent, optimise, reinvent. Rinse and repeat."

— Chisom Okoye, Founder
